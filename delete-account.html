Account Deletion

How to delete your account

You can request the deletion of your AlgoQuest account and all associated data at any time.

Request by email

Send an email to devkostudio@gmail.com with the subject line "Account deletion request" and include the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request within 7 business days.

What data is deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (email, display name)
  • Your progress, XP, streaks, and exercise history
  • Any subscription-related data stored by AlgoQuest
⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Subscription billing must be cancelled separately through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

For any questions, contact us at devkostudio@gmail.com
